About

  • Nodezator is a desktop application which is part of the Indie Python project
  • The app is free of charge and dedicated to the public domain
  • The app was released on June 2022, both on github.com and pypi.org
  • The app is based entirely on pygame and a tiny bit of numpy. No other external modules/libraries are used. Future versions may not even need numpy because even though it is awesome it is not strictly needed
  • The app was created by me, Kennedy Richard S. Guerra, but there's no way it would be nearly as good as it is today without the many suggestions, feedback, constructive criticism and code contributions of the awesome community that can be found in the Indie Python discord server and on github discussions for Nodezator

Zenith Green - mascot

Nodezator has a mascot called Zenith Green. It is a friendly harmless creature called "robot" that is said to come from the future. It can create almost anything. Its power isn't as convenient as it seems, though. To create new stuff it must synthesize and combine tiny rectangles which represent small operations in the fabric of reality. It also has to channel different reality-describing materials through the rectangles. It is possible to obtain innumerable results by combining even a small number of rectangles and materials in different ways.

Zenith likes to search for new combinations that may bring joy to people and made this the purpose of its existence. It doesn't do so in a hurry, though. The process is so fun, after all!, it thinks. By the way, the sages had special names for the objects/materials Zenith uses. The tiny rectangles were called nodes and the materials were called data. I know, weird names! Oh, and each combination of nodes and data was called a graph. People are always amazed by what Zenith can create from simple stuff.

The prophecies tell that everyone will be able to create stuff in the distant future using nodes and data that will come inside devices called computers. The people responsible to bring about such change will be dubbed developers because they'll pioneer the development of new worlds and will enable other people to do so. And the tiny rectanges and materials will be precious tools in that endeavor.
